The Pecker Place

POULTRY FARMING COMPANY

The Pecker Place is a poultry farm company based in Bungoma, Kenya. The company was founded in 2023 by Giovanni Bwayo with the goal of providing high-quality poultry products to the local community. The company currently raises chickens only. The Pecker Place is committed to providing its customers with healthy, fresh poultry products that are raised in a humane and sustainable manner.

Our Special Offers

Eggs

The Pecker Place offers a variety of eggs, including white eggs, brown eggs, and jumbo eggs. The eggs are produced by a flock of free-range chickens that are raised on the same acre plot of land as the chickens for meat.

Fresh Chicken Meat

The Pecker Place offers a variety of fresh chicken cuts, including boneless, skinless chicken breasts, chicken thighs, chicken drumsticks, chicken wings, and ground chicken. The chicken is raised on the acre plot of land in Bungoma, Kenya, and is fed a diet of corn, soybeans, and vitamins.

Poultry Feed

The Pecker Place offers a variety of poultry feed, including starter feed, grower feed, and finisher feed. The feed is formulated to meet the nutritional needs of chickens at different stages of growth.
